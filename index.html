A few projects

Accessibility Curriculum

Over academic year 2017-18, Saba Kawas, Andy Ko, and I conducted a design exploration of faculty professional development at scale. We wanted to figure out how best to support computer science faculty in introducing accessibility topics into their courses. Have a look at the prototype we used for testing.

Binary Games

In the spring of 2017 I worked with Andy Ko and Emilia Gan to investigate the design space of computer science micro-learning possibilities, before deciding to focus on prototyping two games to teach binary number skills. Here's one of the games.

Wikibrain

Over the summer of 2014 I worked with Shilad Sen and Brent Hecht, among others, at the MAXIMA REU. We used Wikibrain, which draws on the wealth of knowledge in Wikipedia, to evaluate the way semantic relatedness, or how similar people believe a pair of words are, is affected by geographic familiarity with the concepts involved (see publications).
